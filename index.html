Designed exclusively for AR/XR glasses like Viture and RayNeo. Seamlessly switch between Flat, 3D, and VR180 content with native head tracking and spatial computing support.

Master Your Media

Intuitive, gaze-friendly controls built for spatial environments. Here is what every button does.

Media & Settings

  • Add Media: Browse local files and folders.
  • Network Stream: Open direct URL video links.
  • Settings: Configure head tracking, codecs, and UI.
  • Premium: Access Pro features and monetization options.

Display Modes

  • Flat 2D Mode: Standard cinema screen viewing.
  • 3D 3D SBS: Side-by-side 3D stereoscopic playback.
  • VR180 Immersive VR: 180-degree spherical video projection.
  • Spatial Tools: Adjust aspect ratio, PiP, screen lock, and left/right eye swap.

Playback & Tracks

  • Play/Pause: Central control for media playback.
  • Skip 10s: Quickly rewind or fast-forward.
  • Audio Tracks: Switch between multiple audio streams (AC3 supported).
  • Subtitles: Select or load custom subtitle files.

Personalize Your Player

Match your style with 6 custom-crafted UI themes. From bright and minimal to dark and immersive.
